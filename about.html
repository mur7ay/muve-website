Our Mission and Vision

We started Muve to alleviate the pain of the shipping process for many online shoppers and businesses. Why do we need to wait days for our packages to arrive when Muve can deliver your items within an hour, seven days a week. Our mission is to really revolutionize the shipping industry and get rid of those long delivery windows, trying to stuff packages into undersized lockers, fumbling with packages on the way home from work, and the fact that 26 million packages get stolen each year. We can all agree that the last mile delivery solutions out there are broken and we set-out to provide a service and platform that puts you back in control. So whether you're making a purchase online or need something moved, you can trust Muve will be there every step of the way with a smiling face to help.

How It All Started

Muve was started on the basis of really simplifying the shipping process. Shipping is the roughage in an entrepreneur's diet and it absolutely consumes to much time for the entrepreneur or their employees. In fact, shipping is one of the most challenging aspects of a business for many entrepreneurs. Muve's co-founder ran into this issue quite often at his previous business when he found himself spending more time packaging and shipping items rather than actually growing the business.
